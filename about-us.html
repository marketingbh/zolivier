About Zipporah

Zipporah is passionate about her vocation. She is committed to applying her skills to help her clients achieve the optimum outcome. Zipporah’s approach is eclectic, drawing from her rich repertoire of training which includes study in psychodynamic, cognitive behaviour, family therapy, couples counselling and solutions focus therapies.

Zipporah brings to her practice her personal dedication to integrity and a lifetime commitment of helping people cope with life challenges. In 2006 Zipporah was awarded the Order of Australia for her honorary work in disability and for her broad-spectrum community service.

Zipporah has an ongoing commitment to her professional development and standard of practice.
Zipporah counsels adults, couples and adolescents.
